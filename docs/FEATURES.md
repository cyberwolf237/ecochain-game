# 🎮 EcoChain Features List

## Core Gameplay Features

### 1. Ecosystem Building
- **Drag-and-Drop System**: Players drag animals/plants onto the ecosystem canvas
- **Food Chain Logic**: Automatic food chain relationships
- **Population Management**: Species reproduce/die based on food availability
- **Resource Management**: Energy, water, nutrients needed for survival

### 2. Dynamic Environment
- **Weather System**: Rain increases water, drought stresses systems
- **Seasonal Changes**: Different challenges per season
- **Time Progression**: Turn-based gameplay (1 turn = 1 day/week)
- **Environmental Events**: Earthquakes, floods, wildfires, etc.

### 3. Learning System
- **Science Facts Database**: 500+ facts about organisms and ecosystems
- **Species Information**: When clicking creatures, learn real facts
- **Educational Quizzes**: Test knowledge after levels
- **Progressive Learning**: Difficulty increases with understanding

### 4. Progression System
- **50+ Levels**: Across 5 different biomes
- **Biome Unlocking**: Progress to unlock new environments
- **Species Unlocking**: New organisms unlock as you advance
- **Sandbox Mode**: Unlimited creation after tutorial

## Content Features

### Biomes (5 Total)
1. **Rainforest** (Tropical ecosystem)
   - High humidity, abundant life
   - Layers: Canopy, understory, forest floor
   - Focus: Biodiversity

2. **Savanna** (Grassland ecosystem)
   - Seasonal rainfall, herbivore dominance
   - Focus: Predator-prey balance
   - Large animal populations

3. **Arctic** (Cold ecosystem)
   - Limited biodiversity
   - Food chain simplicity
   - Focus: Climate impact
   - Fragile balance

4. **Ocean** (Aquatic ecosystem)
   - Phytoplankton to whales
   - Focus: Marine food webs
   - Coral reefs, kelp forests

5. **Desert** (Arid ecosystem)
   - Water scarcity challenge
   - Adapted organisms
   - Focus: Survival strategies

### Species (50+ Total)

**Producers (Plants)**
- Grass, shrubs, trees, kelp, phytoplankton, cactus
- Each biome has 3-5 plant types

**Herbivores**
- Rabbits, deer, zebras, insects, fish
- Each biome has 4-6 herbivores

**Carnivores**
- Wolves, lions, eagles, sharks, snakes
- Each biome has 3-5 predators

**Decomposers**
- Fungi, bacteria, earthworms, vultures
- Each biome has 2-3 decomposers

## Achievement System

### Bronze Achievements (Easy)
- "First Ecosystem" - Build your first ecosystem
- "Predator" - Place a carnivore
- "Green Thumb" - Place 5 plants
- "Survivor" - Complete a level

### Silver Achievements (Medium)
- "Balanced" - Create stable ecosystem (20+ turns)
- "Biome Master" - Complete all levels in a biome
- "Expert Ecosystemer" - Achieve 3-star rating
- "Scientist" - Read 50 species facts

### Gold Achievements (Hard)
- "Earth Guardian" - Get 3 stars on all 50 levels
- "Biodiversity Expert" - Use 30+ different species
- "Perfect Balance" - Create ecosystem lasting 100+ turns
- "Eco Warrior" - Complete all challenges without failure
